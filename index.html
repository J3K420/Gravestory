Location Permission
GraveStory would like to read the GPS location embedded in your gravestone photos to help identify the cemetery and build your personal cemetery map.
Your location data stays on your device and is never uploaded or shared with anyone. It is only used to improve search accuracy and place pins on your personal cemetery map.
How is my data used?
🔒
Your Privacy
What we collect:
GPS coordinates embedded in photos you scan.

What we do with it:
Use it to identify the cemetery location and improve search results. Place a pin on your personal cemetery map.

What we never do:
Upload your location to any server. Share it with third parties. Track your movements.

Where it's stored:
Only on your device, in your browser's local storage. You can clear it any time.
