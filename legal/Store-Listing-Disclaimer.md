# GraveStory — AI Disclaimer Copy (store + in-app reference)

A single place for the AI-disclaimer wording used across the product, so every
surface stays consistent. (NOT legal advice.)

---

## 1. Google Play store listing — short disclaimer line

Add this near the end of your **Full description** in the Play Console (and the
App Store later, if you go iOS). It's transparency for users AND part of
satisfying Play's AI-content expectations.

**Recommended (full):**
> Biographies in GraveStory are generated by AI from public records and historical sources. They may contain errors and are not official or authoritative records. You can report any story directly in the app.

**Shorter (if space is tight):**
> Stories are AI-generated from public records, may contain errors, and are not official records.

**One-liner (e.g. for a promo/subtitle context):**
> AI-assembled remembrance stories — researched from public records, not an official record.

---

## 2. In-app persistent caption (already shipped — for reference)

Shown beneath every generated biography, web + mobile:
> ✦ AI-generated story — researched from public records. It may contain errors and is not an official record. **Report a problem**

## 3. In-app first-view explainer modal (already shipped — for reference)

Title: **About these stories**
Body:
> GraveStory assembles each biography with AI from public records and historical sources. It's a thoughtful starting point for remembrance and research — but it can contain errors and is not an official or authoritative record. If you spot something wrong, you can report it.

Button: **I understand**

## 4. Report sheet copy (already shipped — for reference)

Title: **Report a problem**
Subtitle: *Thanks for helping keep these stories accurate and respectful. What's wrong?*
Reasons: Factual error · Wrong person · Offensive or inappropriate · Privacy concern / about a living person · Something else
Confirmation: *Your report has been sent. We review flagged stories and will take a look.*
