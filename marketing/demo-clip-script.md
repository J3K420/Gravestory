## 3. Recording checklist

**Pick the stone (do this BEFORE filming day)**

- [ ] Choose a strong-record stone: a famous grave, or a locally notable one (a town founder, a
      name from the county history). The clip must show the app at full effect.
- [ ] **Pre-scan it and read the result.** Confirm the story is rich AND the cited-sources list at
      the bottom is long enough to linger on. Never film a thin-bio gamble — if the pre-scan comes
      back short, pick a different stone. (The honest inscription-only fallback is a feature we're
      proud of in reviews; it is not the demo.)
- [ ] **Do NOT make the pre-scan story public** (discard it, or save it private). A public story on
      that grave can be served from the bio cache on the filmed scan, skipping the searching stages
      you need on camera.
- [ ] Scans cost real credits/API — use the unlimited dev account for the pre-scan and the take.

**Android screen-record settings**

- [ ] Record at the phone's highest resolution, 1080p minimum, 30fps+.
- [ ] Do Not Disturb ON for the whole session — no banners, no calls mid-take.
- [ ] Clean status bar: charge the battery high, clear all notification icons, no debug/VPN icons.
- [ ] Turn ON the recorder's "show touches" indicator so viewers can follow the taps.
- [ ] Record device audio OFF (the music bed is added in the edit); mic OFF.
- [ ] Do at least two full takes — scrolling smoothly on camera is harder than it sounds.

**Exterior shot vs pure screen capture**

- Best version: a 3–4 second handheld shot of the phone raised toward the stone (second phone, or
  a friend), then cut to clean screen capture for everything else. The one exterior beat grounds
  the clip in a real cemetery.
- Perfectly acceptable fallback: pure screen capture, opening on shot 2. Don't block the whole
  asset on owning a tripod.

**Landscape vs portrait**

- The **Play listing video is YouTube-hosted and landscape-preferred**. The app is portrait, so
  build the landscape master by centering the portrait screen capture on the app's dark ink
  background (`#14100b`) — clean pillarbox, overlays in the side space if needed.
- The **portrait vertical cut (§2) is for the social variant only** — don't upload it as the Play
  listing video.

**Do-not-show list (check every frame before export)**

- [ ] No legible names on NEIGHBORING stones that could be recent burials — frame tight on the
      chosen stone, or blur neighbors in the edit.
- [ ] No personal notifications anywhere (DND handles this, but check the takes).
- [ ] No account email visible — avoid the Settings screen entirely, and don't film a sign-in.
- [ ] No other cemetery visitors or mourners in the exterior shot.
- [ ] Film respectfully and follow the cemetery's posted rules — the audience for this clip
      notices how you treat the place.
