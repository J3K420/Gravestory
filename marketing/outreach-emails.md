## What every draft deliberately does NOT say (NEVER-SAY compliance)

- Never "AI writes the biography" or leads with "AI" — verified records + citations are the trust device.
- Never "reads the stone" as the *differentiator* — Find A Grave added OCR (Jan 2026); the cited STORY is the claim.
- Never a flat "nobody writes a life story" — CWGC's For Evermore exists; the claim is scoped to "a scan of any *ordinary* person's stone."
- Never "competitors have no map" — Stories in Stone has a private one; we say "public community map."
- Never competitor prices as fact — only our own ($1.99, 3 free) is stated.
- Never implies an iPhone app, faces/photos of the ordinary dead, or a guaranteed rich bio on every stone.
