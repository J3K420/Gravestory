# Play Console Review-Reply Templates — GraveStory

> Copy-paste replies for Google Play reviews, drafted 2026-07-01. The plan layer these serve
> lives in [`gtm-strategy.md`](gtm-strategy.md) (§2 positioning + NEVER-SAY list; §6 notes that
> ratings/reviews are ranking inputs). Fill `[YOUR NAME]` / `[YOUR EMAIL]` before use.

---

## Rules (read before every reply)

- **Reply within 48 hours.** Speed is visible to future visitors, and prompt replies often get
  ratings revised upward — and ratings feed Play ranking.
- **NEVER offer incentives** — no credits, refunds-for-ratings, free scans, or anything in
  exchange for changing or removing a rating. That is a Play policy violation, full stop.
- **Never argue, never blame the user.** Even when they're wrong. Concede what's fair, fix
  what's fixable, take the rest to email.
- **Sign every reply** "— [YOUR NAME], developer".
- **Replies are public and permanent.** Every reply is also marketing copy read by future
  visitors deciding whether to install — write for them as much as for the reviewer.
- **Hard limit: 350 characters.** Counts below are exact for the text as written, placeholders
  included. A real name/email changes the count — re-check after filling in (`[YOUR EMAIL]` is
  12 chars; a longer address eats headroom).
- Stay inside the NEVER-SAY list in `gtm-strategy.md` §2 — no "only AI scanner," no iPhone
  implications, no promised bio depth, no competitor prices.
